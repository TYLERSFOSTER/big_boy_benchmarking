# Glossary

- Schema 0: the matched no-contraction condition.
- Schema 1: selected one-drop noisy-rate quotient candidate.
- Candidate group: one Schema 1 candidate plus its paired Schema 0 condition.
- Sustained hit: a rolling window where at least 4 of 5 episode rewards meet the locked threshold.
- Claim-blocked pair: a pair that cannot support speed comparison because one side failed, never sustained, or hit a structural limit.
